Cooking is chemistry. It is an alchemy that turns simple ingredients into something worth eating. Let's start with something simple!

Fried eggs is a staple of the human diet throughout the world. Below is the easiest method of cooking scrambled eggs.

  • Eggs
  • Bacon Grease
  • Salt
  • Water
  1. Put 2 Tbs Bacon Grease in a heated pan
  2. Scrambe Eggs with touch of Salt and Water
  3. Pour egg mixture into heated pan and cook to desired consistency
